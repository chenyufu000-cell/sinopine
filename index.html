公司介绍 Company Profile

华松国际（SINOPINE INTERNATIONAL）是一家以跨境供应链管理为核心的国际业务平台， 致力于整合全球资源与中国制造优势。

我们以香港与新加坡为枢纽，构建覆盖大宗商品、工业装备（含无人机、发电机组）、 高端消费品等多领域的国际供应链网络，提供从采购、保税加工、跨境物流到渠道分销的一体化解决方案。

秉持“全球视野、价值为本、供应链驱动”的理念，我们通过轻股权、重协同的合作模式， 为企业提供跨境架构设计、市场进入、产业链协同及数字化转型支持， 助力中国制造出海与海外资源引进。

团队拥有平均超过16年的跨境经验，累计运营规模超150亿元， 始终以效率、可靠性与专业服务，为客户创造持续增长的跨境价值。

SINOPINE INTERNATIONAL is a global business platform focused on cross-border supply chain management, integrating global resources with the strengths of Chinese manufacturing.

With Hong Kong and Singapore as strategic hubs, we have built an international supply chain network covering bulk commodities, industrial equipment (including drones and generator sets), and premium consumer products. We provide integrated solutions from sourcing and bonded processing to cross-border logistics and channel distribution.

Guided by the philosophy of “Global Vision, Value-Oriented, Supply Chain Driven”, we adopt a light-equity, high-collaboration model to support enterprises in cross-border structure design, market entry, industrial chain coordination, and digital transformation, facilitating both the global expansion of Chinese manufacturing and the introduction of overseas resources.

Our team has an average of over 16 years of cross-border experience, with cumulative operations exceeding RMB 15 billion in scale. We consistently create sustainable cross-border value for clients through efficiency, reliability, and professional service.
